About Gauri Precision Tools

Company Overview

Established in the year 2009, Gauri Precision Tools is a leading Manufacturer, Wholesaler, Trader and Service Provider of precision engineered components such as Mild Steel Shafts, Motor Shafts and many more.

With over 17 years of industry experience, we have built a strong reputation for quality, reliability and customer satisfaction.
